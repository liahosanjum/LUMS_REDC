Developing Future Leaders

 

 

 

PROGRAMME BENEFITS

 

 

Organisations that invest in leadership development perform better than those that don't. Challenging times magnify this fact even more. Studies show that, investments in leadership development significantly;


• Improve bottom-line financial performance
• Attract & retain talent
• Drive a performance culture
• Increase organisational agility

 

The objective of this programme is to impart to the participants the contemporary knowledge and skills to become effective in their roles as future leaders of their organisations. The programme will focus on developing greater confidence, creativity, resourcefulness, initiative, and solutions-finding and problem-solving capabilities of the potential leaders so that they can operate at higher strategic level to make their respective organisations more productive and competitive.

 

 

 

 

KEY LEARNING OBJECTIVES

 

 

• Gain an understanding of your leadership style and improve your interpersonal skills
• Contribute to collaborative performance through effective team building and management
• Understand the importance and applicability of 360-degree feedback in identifying your future training and development needs
• Effectively communicate your goals and objectives, and influence others positively
• Learn effective and timely decision making , goal setting, and problem solving skills
• Develop understanding of the key role of customer centric culture and its importance in sustainable advantage
• Learn the key business financials and their impact on enhancing financial health of an organisation
• Develop your capabilities to enhance quality of outputs and to drive organisational innovation
• Acquire practical learning to become a skillful leader through a state-of -the-art leadership simulation exercise

WHO SHOULD ATTEND

 

 

The programme is ideal for the mid-level potential managers with 5-8 years of work experience who:

• Are high achievers, on the fast-track with high potential
• Have the confidence and courage to provide necessary changes in the organisations
• Are motivated to invest in reinforcing their leadership potential with a strong foundation of skills and knowledge
• Are being groomed as functional heads/team leaders
• Are functional heads/team leaders
