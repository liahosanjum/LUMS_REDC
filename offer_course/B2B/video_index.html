Business to Business (B2B) Marketing

December 8 – 10, 2009  

 

Benefits

B2B is currently one of the fastest growing area of marketing and has emerged as a discipline in its own right. The programme addresses the essential need of a business to strategically engineer and cement its ties with other businesses it transacts with. In today’s fast paced environment, competitiveness of a business is often based on the quality and turnaround time of the delivered output. This is determined not only by, the firm’s own efficiency but also from the effectiveness of its demand and supply channels. This programme develops a practical orientation of operating in a B2B environment and will provide tools, insights and techniques required to buy and sell effectively as a B2B marketer.

Benefits

·         Managing Products in a B2B Environment

·         Market Research in B2B Context

·         Attractivity/Inverted Marketing (Managing Supply-Demand Chain)

·         Role & Function of Decision Making Units (DMUs)

·         Understanding & Managing Channels

·         Product & Pricing Strategies in a B2B Environment
